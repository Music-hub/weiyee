Musichub
Music Hub

Musichub is a online sheet music editor which supports co-edition and live play.You can co-edit the works with your band-members or other creator by using Musichub.You can get inspired by viewing or listening others' work on Musichub.Musichub provides a lot of useful tool,for example,chord tool,which can help you improve the working efficiency.Musichub also provides live play.After selecting the desired instrument sounds,you can play your works or listen other creators' music.
